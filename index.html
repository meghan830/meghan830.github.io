My Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookie             

Ingredients:
2/3 cup vegetable shortening
2/3 cup softened butter
1 cup light brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Blend shortening, butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
3. Add eggs and vanilla to mixture and combine thoroughly.
4. In a seperate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
5. Combine flour mixture with fat/sugar mixture.
6. Add chocolate chips to dough.
7. Roll dough into balls and place on parchment-lined baking sheets.
8. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the cookies' edges are golden brown.
9. Let cool and enjoy!
